Privacy Policy

Quran & Prayer · Last updated 25 June 2026

Siraj is a privacy-first Islamic app. There is no account, no advertising, no analytics SDK, and no tracking of any kind. The Quran text, translation, transliteration, tafsir commentary, prayer-time calculation, Qibla direction, duas, Tasbih, the 99 Names, the Hijri calendar, and the memorization (Hifz) tools all run entirely offline on your device.

What we collect

Nothing about you is collected on a server, because we operate no server that receives your personal data. Siraj has no sign-in. The notes, bookmarks, highlights, memorization progress, and settings you create stay on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app.

Location

Location (approximate, foreground-only) is requested for a single purpose: to calculate accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction for where you are. Your coordinates are used on your device and cached locally so the app keeps working offline; they are never uploaded to us or shared with anyone. Resolving a city name for display may use your device's operating-system geocoder.

Notifications

Notifications are requested only if you keep adhan (prayer-time) alerts enabled. They are scheduled locally on the device with no network round-trip.

Audio recitation (the only optional online feature)

When you tap play on an ayah, the app streams that recitation from everyayah.com, a free public Quran-audio host; your request for that audio file goes to that host. No personal data is attached. All other content, including tafsir, is bundled in the app and needs no connection.

The optional one-time "Study Tools" purchase

Siraj is free, with no subscriptions and no ads. A single optional one-time purchase unlocks the Study Tools (verse-by-verse tafsir, every reciter, and offline audio downloads). Payment is processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store billing system, and we use RevenueCat to validate and remember the unlock. RevenueCat receives only an anonymous app-generated identifier and your entitlement (unlocked or not) — not your name, your location, or anything you read or memorize in the app.

Permissions

Siraj only requests a device permission when a feature you use requires it — location for prayer times and Qibla, and notifications for adhan alerts. Each is described above.

Children's privacy

Siraj is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. There is no account and no user-generated content shared with others.

Data security

Because your data never leaves your device, its security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, OS-level encryption). Any network requests Siraj makes (audio streaming, purchase validation) use HTTPS.

Your choices

You control everything locally: turn notifications or location off in your device settings at any time, and uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data. Because we hold no personal data on a server, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.
