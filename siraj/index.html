Quran & Prayer — private, offline, ad-free

Siraj is a privacy-first Islamic companion: the complete Quran with translation and transliteration, verse-by-verse tafsir, accurate prayer times with adhan, the Qibla compass, duas, Hifz memorization, the 99 Names, and the Hijri calendar — working fully offline, with no ads, no trackers, and no data ever sold.

Available in Arabic, English, Indonesian, Urdu, Turkish, French, Bengali, and Malay.

Common questions

  • Do I need an account? No. Siraj has no sign-in and works entirely on your device.
  • Why does it ask for location? Only to calculate prayer times and the Qibla for where you are — it never leaves your phone.
  • Is it free? Yes. An optional one-time "Study Tools" purchase unlocks offline tafsir and every reciter; everything essential for worship is free forever. No subscriptions.
  • How do I change the language? Pick it on first launch, or switch anytime in Settings → Language.
